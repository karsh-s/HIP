Our Goal 
Our
      Goal
Unlike most of us, people with hearing impairment and those who are deaf are not able to enjoy music the same way as us. We can jam to the music by simply playing it through our phone. Deaf people on the other hand have to either blast it on specially made headphones or feel the music through the vibrations of the floor or speaker. Our goal is to make the process of listening and playing music as easy as possible for deaf people as it is for us.

The Music Player 
Music
      Player
The Music Player is meant to send an output to the Haptic Sensor on the Ardunio (wristband). The Arduino Sensor is then meant to pick up the song and vibrate the haptic sensor based on the beat. We have made the website on our side but with more support, we can connect the arduino to perform the actions listed above. Click this paragraph or page title to redirect to the music player.
